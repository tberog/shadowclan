The BloodMoon Tribe, one of the first, was formed by the Wise UnsGub in the early days of the Clan. Orcs in this tribe tend to be fervently religious and devoted to the Wargod. This religious mindset makes them more introspective than most orcs. Having a certain calm, inner peace is important to them, and they will often go on pilgrimages or make sacrafices when they feel this peace is disturbed. This calmness is evident in battle, where they will cooly engage the enemy, hacking at them with a strange gleam in their eyes while chanting prayers and devotions to the Wargod.

The BloodMoon Tribe is a group of zealot Orcs banding together and motivated to carry out the Wargod’s will and spread his message. When converting, they will either succeed or die. It is all the same to a "moonie".

The BloodMoon tribe firmly believes that all power springs from the heart, and thus they tend to collect hearts of all kinds, epecially their own. Some will even drink blood from a fresh victim. Right and wrong are not as important as the Wargod's will. They follow the Wargod and his orders blindly and stop at nothing to get the job done. Accidents will be attributed to the Will of the Wargod.

BloodMoon also place more value in their beliefs than in their own life which can make them very dangerous. Moonies consider themselves to be close to the Wargod and hence superior to others. They disregard all others' opinions as the Wargod is the only one capable of valid reasoning. This Wargod pride often leads them to be greedy, arrogant, intolerant and egocentric.

The Power of the Wargod continues to grow and the Bloodmoon tribe can be found eagerly collecting tribute for the Wargod. Another of BloodMoon's missions is to spread the Wargod Insanity plague to all Orcs and non-orcs. Orcs have been blessed with the power of Insanity. Every non-believer should be given the opportunity to hear about the Wargod and become a Convert. Human conversion involves death of the human flesh so the spirit can visit the Wargod and be given Orcish flesh. Non-believers should be clomped so their spirit can visit the Wargod personally where the Wargod's direct influence can attempt another Conversion. Repeat attempts at conversion with some stubborn unbelievers is not unheard of. Sometime the spirit is still human in the Orcish flesh and clomping of gruntees is not unheard of. Any time a gruntee is clomped for such a reason, he should be informed that he might rejoice in the cleansing.

The Wargod talks to many and is hard to interrpate. The reception and interpretation of the Wargod’s plans are currently left up to the Mojoka, Nobs and occassional enlightened Orc. When the Wargod needs action BloodMoon will do it. BloodMoon following the Wargod’s directions will acquire objects of power or clomp the Wargod's enemies. A BloodMooner prides himself at being the best Luskr, Wompr, kultr, zultr, and Ligzr in the Clan.

" The power of the Wargod is growing. Not only has the strength and numbers of the Orcs grown, but also their influence over humanity is gaining. The Orcs now have power to cause insanity. It appears after some social contact with Orcs some humans are corrupted. These weak-willed humans have been known to babble in the orcish language. They are also tricked by Orcs into many evil acts against their own race. Orcs love to see humans fight humans. Orcs still do not trust these insane humans but tolerate them for short periods of time. The Wargod influences these insane humans to further his cause which makes the ShadowClan stronger. As the ShadowClan grows so to does the Wargod's influence over the common humans that populate the land... "

- UnsGub, Nob of BloodMoon

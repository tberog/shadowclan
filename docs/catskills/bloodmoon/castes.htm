Each BloodMoon orc has his own way of serving the Wargod but many choose to align themselves within a certain caste within the tribe. There are four seperate Castes within the BloodMoon tribe.

A Caste is chosen by a Convert and either the top rank of the Caste or a Nob sends the Convert on an appropriate quest. After the quest, the Convert is told whether he is accepted into the Caste or not. If rejected, he may choose another caste right away, or he may reapply for the same one after a month passes.

An orc who wishes to switch castes must write a story about it and have some in-game interaction that relates to his story and caste switching (details to be worked out by the orc in question).

This is by no means meant to tell you how to play your orc. They are merely some suggestions and those who don't wish to participate in the intra-tribe rankings are not forced to. Expect promotions to be handed out very infrequently. Only through LONG hard work and true devotion to the Wargod, Tribe and Clan can an orc expect to achieve the top ranks. Promotions based upon RP ability, devotion and sacrifice to the Moonies and the Clan, contributions to the orc experience and the amount of respect you get from other orcs and even other roleplayers.

 

Fanatic Caste: These are orcs who are completely and utterly devoted to the Wargod and the Moonies. They will often try to protect other orcs in their tribe, feeling they should take the brunt of any combat. They are ferocious in battle, but realize that living to fight another day is sometimes the wiser course. Fanatics often believe that the cause of any losing battle is the anger of the Wargod. This makes them feel guilty and they will take whatever steps they feel appropriate to obtain the favor of the Wargod again.
Titles: Fanatic - Dervish - Holy Warrior - Arm of de Wargod

Martyr Caste: These are orcs who are completely and utterly devoted to the Wargod beyond anything else. They are fearless in battle, no matter what the odds and will fight to the death defending the honor of the Moonies and the Wargod. They view death as just another chance to see the Wargod and are quite often disappointed when a battle doesn't lead to their own death. Martyrs fully believe in the Power of the Wargod over everything. Anything that occurs, good or bad, is the Will of the Wargod. They will often work themselves into a battle fervor before a fight, chanting and mumbling prayers and devotions to the Wargod. Upon gaining the first rank they are taught to read battle scars.
Titles: Martyr - Devotee - Zealot - Hand of de Wargod

Preacher Caste: These are orcs who are devoted to spreading the Word of the Wargod, to both orcs and non-orcs. They tend to be a little more philosophical than most orcs and can often be found shouting out the Words of the Wargod, whether anyone is listening or not. Many a time has a human warrior been distracted by a Preacher yelling about the Wargod as he fights. Orcs of this caste tend to lead many preaching trips to human settlements.
Titles: Preacher - Missionary - Disciple - Mouth of de Wargod

Prophet Caste: These orcs are almost detached from everyday orc life. They will often have dreams and visions of the Wargod and will sometimes consume plants and herbs in an attempt to cause even more visions. They believe that they have been chosen by the Wargod and are responsible for trying to interpret the dreams and visions, both their own and those of other orcs.
Titles: Prophet - Seer - Oracle - Voice of de Wargod

Convert: These are tribeless orcs that understand the Ways of the Wargod and wish to be Moonies to help further his will. This title also applies to orcs from other tribes who have seen the light and wish to join the esteemed ranks of the Moonies.
Titles: Convert

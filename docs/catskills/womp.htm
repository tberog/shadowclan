The Womp-R Bludline

Mace & Shield wielding orcs
Good at bashing rocks
Follow the way of Ogba (Brute Force)

The strongest bludline of Shadowclan, they rely on strength and size to overpower an enemy, 

using their shield to pummel rather than block.  They also mine most of the clan's ore.

Biggest advantages are strength and damage done to enemy armor.  The shield adds extra protection.

Disadvantages are slower speed due to low dexterity and stamina, and weapons do less damage.

Proficiency skills - Mace Fighting, Parrying, Mining.

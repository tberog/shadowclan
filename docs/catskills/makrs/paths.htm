Mak'r Rules -- Urucku and Ranks -- Mak'r Paths

Paths: General
At Nubkuu rank, a Mak'r must choose a main path he/she wants to pursue. This rank denotes both the primary purpose of service he/she provides to Shadowclan, as well as what items he/she may weild.

All Mak'rs may weild a pickaxe or lantern of any color.

Paths: Special
At Tala rank, a Mak'r may ask for a quest for approval to be part of a unique path. The quest involves writing a thoughtful story that explains the character's change to the special path and how it fits into the role play of that Mak'r. For the Forj'r and Chizzl'r path, it is expected that the Mak'r is already capable of supplying services involved in that skill and has integrated it into his/her role play over time.

Drum'r:
Musicianship and Provoke and/or Peacemaking and/or Discordance. After completing the quest and receiving approval, a Mak'r may take up a drum and go about learning the ways of music. It is up to the Mak'r what additional skills he/she wishes to take in relation to this path. He/she is encouraged to use music on resource trips and in defense of shardies at the fort.

Grind'r:
Alchemy. May take up to 50 Fencing and 50 Tactics. These Mak'rs take up regeants and discover the many uses for them. They are responsible for providing potions to Shadowclan. They may be shady characters in league with Kult'rs, so beware.

Forj'r:
Tinkering, Blacksmithing, and Mining. May take up to 50 Macing and 50 Tactics. Having perfected the usage of ore in a variety of ways, a Forj'r is named after proving his/her worth and knowledge of the movement and crafting of ore in its different forms.

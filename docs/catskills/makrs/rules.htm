Mak'r Rules -- Urucku and Ranks -- Mak'r Paths

Rules: Respect and Teamwork

Combat
Mak'rs are allowed to walk around armed using the weapons of the appropriate Mak'r Path, but you are not allowed to play your Mak'r as a "warrior" character. Use your Grunt for such role play.

You are not allowed to start a combat fight with a Grunt or higher (exceptions are Mog'ob'goth, Sharkuu and Big Mak'r).

Mak'rs are not highlighted to any guilds. This is to ensure that Mak'rs can safely visit towns to gather BODs and receive healing aid from orcs on the Shadowclan Orcs Gakh guildstone. Do not use your non-highlighted status to get away with taunting any guilds Shadowclan Orcs are warred with.

You are allowed to go on trips with the exception of if it is a trip to kill highlighted guilds.

Ranks
Respect chain of command. At no time are you allowed to refuse a Nob or Blood Leader the use of your services or refuse to obey their direct order.

You will obey and respect those Mak'rs higher ranked than you.

Do not deny any orc your services over Tribal differences.

Resources, BODs, and Vendors
Mak'rs are encouraged to collect BODs and store them at the Mak'r Hut. All BOD rewards go to benefit Shadowclan Orcs and trusted Mak'rs will be given the rewards (powerscrolls, runic sewing kits, runic hammers) to use in appropriate ways.

Replenish resources that you borrow, or inform the Big Mak'r of resources you have taken which you cannot replenish (ex. if you cannot mine Valorite but use some to make an orc an item). Public resources will be kept in the Mak'r hut beside the fort. Backstock will be kept in Leadership access.

Orcs at Buorca rank may place a vendor in a house near the fort and should be themed (ex. Prel runs a Bang'r snagga, Kragnagh runs a Stich'r snagga). All gold earnings must be reported monthly to the Big Mak'r and vendors must be emptied out of gold at the first of every month, then passed on in check or pile form to the Big Mak'r. Gold will go to supplies for Mak'rs and Shadowclan.

 

Skills and Powerscrolls
Powergaming is not allowed. Create role playable situations to make items (ex. making forts out of benches and crates).

Unattended macroing, which is a bannable offense to OSI, will result in the removal of your Mak'r and all Grunt characters from Shadowclan.

Powerscrolls at the appropriate rank may be taken as long as the Big Mak'r is aware. If you wish to have a skills above your designated rank, you may seek promotion as a method to allow higher skill.
Tala: +5 powerscrolls
Buorca: +10 powerscrolls
Durub: +15 powerscrolls
Mog'Ob'Goth/Big Mak'r: +20 powerscrolls


Other
All Mak'rs are allowed to have beetles. It is recommended that you have a beetle rather than a pack animal to avoid the threat of shardies. However, do not ride the beetle unless given permission by a Leader or in a situation where no orcs are present and you must save the beetle from monster attacks.

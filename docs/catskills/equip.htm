Equipment Summary

 

Each rank gains progressively better equipment.

Nobs can be identified by their sashes.

Nobs may wear bone armor, but must wear bone hands if wearing bone arms.

Bloodline leaders wear bone arms with non-bone gloves.


Guards can wear bone legs, and utilize additional weapons.

Colored weapons are permitted, but they must be orc made.

Colored armor permitted only with specific approval of a nob or blood leader.

Non-Mak-R orcs may use Bolas with Bludline leader approval.

 

 

Current and future player craftable magic armor and weapons are restricted as follows:

If the item appears in either the 2D or 3D client as some item that is not normally

allowable to orcs (plate armor, swords, etc.), then its use will not be allowed.

 

If the item appears in both clients as some item normally allowable to orcs, then its

use will be allowed provided it is crafted by a Shadowclan character. If the item can

carry a maker's mark, then that mark must be of a Shadowclan character. 

 

Bludline leaders have the option of making bludline specific restriction in regards to

use of any items. When in doubt consult your Bludline leader.

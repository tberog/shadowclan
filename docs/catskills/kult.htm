The Kult-R Bludline

Kryss wielding assasin orcs
Follow the way of Aughagh (Deception)

 

Secretive and elusive.  Even members of Shadowclan are smart to avoid a Kult-R.  

The Kult-R uses surprise and stealth to their advantage to kill silently and quickly.

Biggest advantages is the ability to mix and use greater and deadly poisons.  Kulthu rank allowed to use disguises.

Disadvantages are being a bit vulnerable if the assassination attempt fails.  Can not attain warboss or guard rank.

Proficiency skills - Proficiency skills - Poisoning, Fencing, Alchemy, Tinkering, Detect Hidden, Stealth

 

Note that due to the nature of this bludline, the number of kult-r in the clan are limited.  

To become a kult-r, you must first have an active grunt in the clan and then apply for a position

The K'igg-R Bludline

Spear wielding orcs
Medic orcs, good at bandaging up wounds
Follow the way of Iggju (Quickness/Tactics)

Tall, thin, lanky average-strengthed bludline of Shadowclan orcs, they rely on 

quickness to strike an enemy faster than the enemy can strick them.  Their superior knowledge 

of healing arts make them invaluable for repairing wounds, curing poisons and ressurecting.

Biggest advantages are speed and weapon damage.

Disadvantages are lower strength.

Proficiency skills - Fencing, Healing, Anatomy, Parrying

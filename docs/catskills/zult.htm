The Zult-R Bludline

Scimitar & Shield wielding orcs
Good at fishing
Follow the way of Rokgagh (Deceptive Tactics)

The newest bludline, they migrating from the Lost Lands and swore allegiance to Shadowclan. 

Known for battle skills and their treachery, as well as their fish.

Biggest advantages are balance of speed and strength, and a shield that adds extra protection.  Never run out of food.

Primary disadvantages is weaker weapon damage.

Proficiency skills - Swordsmanship, Parrying, Fishing.

Purpose

To have fun and provide some fun for those that oppose us.   It may not always be like this but the current UO world has many a player who has nothing to do.   They have maxed out, have all the gold they could ever need and find little enjoyment in the game.  We hope to provide a little role-played rivalry between us and the humans in the area.   We have developed into an active round the clock player run orc village based out of an orc fort.  We seek to defend our land rights against others with who we are at war.

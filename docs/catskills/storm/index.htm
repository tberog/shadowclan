STORY

From boats made by Throquat paws, a horde of orcs swarmed the docks of Serpent's Hold. The many Tribes were strong together, followed by Tribeless and Gruntees ready for the pillage. The unprepared townsfolk were slaughtered, few left as prisoners. The Byzantines, who had found perfection in Serpent's Hold as a new place to defend and live, grew alert as billows of smoke rolled from burning hay roofs in the main town. The army set up at the chapel as the orcs gathered in the archery butte territory to spread out their loot and the bodies of the dead. Rain fell from the sky of heavy gray clouds, lightning flashed and thunder rolled. Battle stirred in the hearts of beasts and man.

RULES

Ghosts must remain entirely away from fighting for the thirty minutes after gathering the items from their bodies. Ghost robes must be worn for the full time. Ghosts report to their appropriate locations. Human ghosts wait in the specified blue building, and orcs wait in the specified red building. See the map below for location details.

Ghosts should be weary and wear their robes on the way to their base, then derobe at their base. They cannot wear robes longer than is necessary at their own base.

No ghost spying.

Main battles will be led on the orc side by the Warboss and on the human side by the Byzantine leader. Skirmishes may be led by any interested and present character. Once a battle, skirmish, or other interaction is complete, it is important to return to one's own base and not camp the other guilds' territory.

It is encouraged to do more than fight. Be clever about confrontation. Attempt to have some face to face talk or exchange before the attack begins. This is to avoid KoS'ing of any characters.

The Mojoka Bludline

Magic wielding orcs
Follow the way of Ghigneh (Closeness to Wargod)

Weak and odd, to most orcs the Mojoka's of Shadowclan seem to be able to predict when the Wargod will aid the orcs (magic).  

A mojoka that is close to the wargod at the scene of a battle can often be the difference between victory and defeat.

Biggest advantage is the ability to use magic and related skills.

Disadvantages are extreme vulnerability to melee combat, and the necessity of reagents.  Can not attain warboss or guard rank.

Proficiency skills - Magery, Necromancy, Evaluate Intelligence, Spirit Speak, Meditation, Alchemy, Inscription

Note that since orcs in general fear magic, the number of mojoka in the clan are limited.  

To become a mojoka, you must first have an active grunt in the clan and then apply for a position.

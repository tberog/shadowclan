Rules for Gruntees

A gruntee should not go to humie towns unless a higher ranked orc takes them there.
A gruntee should not use banks.
A gruntee should obey all orders by higher ranked orc.
A gruntee should never touches corpses of other players, unless ordered to by higher ranked orc (If you double click a corpse, your name will show up when Forensics Evaluation is used)
A gruntee should never loot any type of corpses when a grunt or higher ranked orc is around.
A gruntee should not talk to humies if a higher ranked orc is there talking to them
A gruntee should not use unattended macroing for skill or stat gain, or exploit bugs (no member of Shadowclan is allowed to do those things)
A gruntee should not use magic items
A gruntee should not hoard gold.  Try giving gold to grunts and nobs you want to impress.
A gruntee should not attack other players that highlight blue (this can cause a lot of problems in the long run)
A gruntee should follow the clan's guildwar rules.

 

Rules for Grunts

A grunt should avoid human towns, unless escorting a nob, makr, or mojoka
A grunt should obey all orders by higher ranked orc.
A grunt should never loot any type of corpse when a  higher ranked orc is around and looting the corpse.
A grunt should not use unattended macroing for skill or stat gain, or exploit bugs (no member of Shadowclan is
allowed to do those things)
A grunt should not abuse his authority.

All grunts are allowed to ressurect using bandages. 

All passive magic items that grant skills, stats, or bonuses of any sort are approved for use by grunts. 

 

 

 

Guildwar Rules

Stay in character.
Show respect for your enemies, do not try to ruin their fun.
Try to avoid becoming criminals or murderers around the clan fort.
Will follow the simplified rules of engagement (SRoE)
 
The simple rules of engagement (SRoE) are:
 (1) do not attack players that obviously have lost connection,
 (2) after resurrecting, a player remains in death robes, and doesn't talk, and doesn't participate further in
that battle (or for 30 minutes, whichever is shorter). Death robed players that follow this rule are not to be
attacked. Death  robed players may loot their own corpses.

 

Looting Rules

(1) Gruntees do not touch highlighted bodies.
(2) No orcs loot highlighted bodies unless some compelling roleplayed reason exists to do so, or if the situation warrants pre-emptive looting in order to protect a person’s items from 3rd party looters or decay.
(3) In the case of NPC or non-highlighted kills, the highest ranked orc present is in charge of looting. No other orc touches the body unless directed to do so by that ranking orc.  

(4) Loot only things YOUR OWN orc can use (food, drinks including potions, gold, potions, arrows if ligzr, reagents if Mojoka, anything taken from the orc, weapon if it is allowed by your bludline, nobs can loot anything any orc uses).  In some circumstances you should also take away the victim's fighting capability (weapons, arrows, reagents) if they are a threat to continue the fight.

 

Punishment

Depending on the rule violated, punishment may involve having certain items confiscated, having a bad report sent to the bludline leader, being clomped, being demoted, inventive punishments, and/or in very very severe cases being clomped to death by a bloodline leader or nob.  Having bad reports sent to your bloodline leader can delay your promotion to grunt.

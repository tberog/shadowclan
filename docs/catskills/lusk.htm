The Lusk-R Bludline

Axe wielding orcs
Good at chopping down trees
Follow the way of Kihagh (Bloodlust)

The first and most storied bludline of Shadowclan, they cut and slice through enemy ranks without worrying about self preservation.

Biggest advantage is axes are easy to replace if lost, just take one from an NPC orc lord.

Disadvantage is they are completely offensively focused.

Proficiency skills - Swordsmanship, Lumberjacking, Parrying

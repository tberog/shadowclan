The Ligz-R Bludline

Bow & Arrow wielding orcs
Good at Tracking
Follow the way of Gagru (Deceptive Might)

Small, weak, and quick, this bludline in Shadowclan serves as a scout type orc.  Their keen sense of smell makes them good at Tracking.  Though small, they are effective in battle and especially fort defense, as their use of the bow allows them to strike from a distance.

Biggest advantage is the ability to strike from a distance.  The ability to track enemies and detect hidden enemies is also useful.

Disadvantages are extreme vulnerability to melee combat, and the necessity of arrows to fight.  Can not wear ringmail armor.  

Can not attain warboss or guard ranks.  May obtain Fort Guard rank.

Proficiency skills - Archery, Tracking, Detect Hidden, Bowcraft/Fletching, Lumberjacking

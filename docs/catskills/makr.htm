The Mak-R Bludline

Supply orcs
Good at making things
Follow the way of Buorca (Selfless teamwork)

Various shapes and sizes this bludline in Shadowclan is responsible for supplying and repairing weapons, armor, tools, and other necessities to the rest of the clan.  Their selfless attitude allows them to work endlessly while the warriors in the clan focus on war.

Biggest advantage is being able to craft many items while other orcs gather raw materials.

Disadvantages are lack of combat skill, making them ineffective fighters.  Can not attain warboss or guard rank.

Proficiency skills - Blacksmithy, Tinkering, Tailoring, Carpentry, Bowcraft/Fletching, Cooking, Arms Lore, Mining, Lumberjacking

Advanced Language

Some members may wish to explore the Irekei language further. It is by no means required and members are asked to use some common sense in their usage of the advanced language — the language is not meant to be a barrier to effective general communication between members.

There are three places to find further information on the advanced language:

  1. SC Irekei language list
  2. This thread on the Shadowclan forums.
  3. SB Lore's archive of Gradishar's 4 letters

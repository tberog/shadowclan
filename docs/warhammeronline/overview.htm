What Shadowclan Greenskins are About

  • Roleplay - Shadowclan Greenskinss are always in character.
  • Open Membership - Anyone can create a Greenskins following character creation guidlines and join.
  • Community - Greenskinss fight together, live together and die together. Everything belongs to the Clan, everyone shares supplies freely amongst themselves.
  • Game Friendly - Our storyline and background blend in with the game's storyline and background to the highest degree possible. We won't force other players to break character in order to match our own role play style. We follow the rules of the game. No Cheating, bug exploitation, illegal 3rd party programs, or other violations of the Terms of Service are ever allowed.
  • Commitment - In order to remain part of Shadowclan after an initial trial period, a Greenskins must permanently commit their characters to Shadowclan.
  • Respect - We respect other players as fellow players.
  • Making the game more fun - We have fun and try to increase the fun for others by providing an intelligent enemy as an alternative to NPC's
  • Quality - We train our new members in the Shadowclan ways and our expectaions are enforced.

Want to make an alternate character?

Shadowclan is originally a guild for alternate characters (Ultima Online 1997 "Lets all make orc characters") and here in Age of Conan, Cimmeria Server, the branch is designed to be a great guild for alternate characters ("Lets all make Greenskins characters"), offering an alternative style of playing the game that you rarely find in other guilds.

Grouping


Whenever possible group with other Shadowclan members.  We do not expect you to kick out non clan members that you are already in a group with to bring in a Shadowclan member but we do expect you to ask your guild mates first if they would like to come before joining or forming a group.


Sharing Items


Equipment, supplies, and money are shared among the clan.  If you have an item that is green quality or above, before vendoring it, first ask in guild chat if anyone needs it.  If you know of someone who is of the appropriate level that could possibly use the item, preferably mail it to them.  If you can share with your guild members do so.

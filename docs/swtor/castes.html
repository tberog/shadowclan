The Castes

To become a full member of Shadowclan one can join one of the castes.  

The castes are:

The Adassi  

The priest caste.  These are members of the cult who follow the spiritual teachings of Adas.  They can be, of course, very cruel but tend to be more level headed.

The Massassi

 The warrior caste.  Those who seek out the enemies of the Sith’ari and kill them where they stand.   Obviously, they are more aggressive.

The Jenassi

 The assassin caste.  Those who can disappear into the shadows and not only gather intelligence for the cult but assassinate its enemies.

There are also the casteless; those who do not feel that their path falls into either of the three traditional castes of the cult.

Want To Try Us Out?

You must create a new character or have an existing character under level 15 that meets the character creation requirements below. Shadowclan does not accept any characters over level 15 into it's membership.

You will need to choose the server _________________

Character Creation

The races available to you are either a Sith, Human or Cyborg. The choice stems from our background story. We are a clan of pure bloods and their human descendants.

Skin Color

a) If you choose human or cyborg then your character comes from a family of Sith. His or her blood is too diluted to show the red skin. (Note that this is a role play background; not a game mechanic.)

b) your character comes from a family with Sith heritage and raised in Sith traditions,  ideals while appearing human. (Note that this is a role play background; not a game mechanic.)

Class Restrictions

There are no class restrictions, as they are all covered by the three races above.

 

Naming Your Character

Shadowclan is known for their immersive role-play and do not allow non role play names. If you are unsure if your name will be accepted, please contact one of the members online to verify it. You can also post on the forums and ask if the name is acceptable or not.

Select an appropriate name. Shadowclan is known for their immersive role-play and does not allow non role-play names.

  • Your name should not contain any special characters or accents over letters.
  • Apostrophe’s are ok to use.

If you are unsure if your name will be accepted, please contact one of the members online to verify it. You can also post on the forums and ask if the name is acceptable or not. The general rule of thumb is if it is attached to "real life" or sounds like a real life person, place or thing it is not allowed.

Examples of names not allowed:

Real Names: Steve, Leroy Jenkins, Susie, Kendall

Cutsie Names: Wookinookins, Leezard Imapants

Converted Names/Words: Mirahaj (Mirage), Chyna (China)

 

Asking For An Invitation To Join In The Game

Log in and find out who is online from the guild. Ask for an invite.  The person doing the invite will check to make sure that your character meets the requirements.  As well, you will be asked about the language for the clan and a few words will be reviewed.  If you haven’t reviewed the language section please do so.

Level Restrictions

There is a level restriction as well.  We do not accept characters over level 15 who have not yet joined the guild and do not have a compelling reason as to why they didn’t. Shadowclan has a communal guild structure and community and we want characters to grow with us and learn about us.  If the player decides that this is not the right place for them, then the most they can lose is a low level character with a small time investment. For both reasons, characters over level 15 will not be accepted.

Become Familiar With Our Member Expectations

Read our expectations in the members section, play and have fun.

Name:

You have two options, use the random name generator within Everquest or you can try to come up with something that sounds good and monster-like. Check the Current Clan Roster for examples. Consult the Shadowclan language section if you want to give your name some meaning. Do not use a name that has any meaning to humans. You will NOT be allowed to join the clan with an improper name.

Race & Class:

Starting characters can be of any of the four evil races (Dark Elf, Iksar, Troll, or Ogre) or the neutral Vah Shir. Class can be any class allowable by the race you choose. Such is the way of Evil.

Gender:

Stats:

Shadowclan monsters are strong and durable. Most are not known for their intelligence as those tending towards cerebral pursuits are usually the first to be eaten during lean times. Those who do possess a certain animal cunning and physical prowess are generally the leaders. For the trolls and ogres, putting your additional points into strength and stamina are highly recommended.

[To Top]

Skills:

It is highly recommended that the warriors develop their taunting skills from an early age on. Even in one on one combat, it is always good to insult your prey before squashing him. This will make you much better suited to grouping with the spell casters at later levels. Spell casters, upon hitting 4th level... GRAB MEDITATE SKILL and drop at least one point into it. You will use it frequently to regain mana, memorize quicker, and scribe faster. Most other skill issues will take care of themselves in game.

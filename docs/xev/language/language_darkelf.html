Dark Elf Dictionary

As the intellectual leaders of Shadowclan, it is the duty of each individual Tier’dall to be fluent in his or her native tongue.

That said, it is also necessary to be understanding of the limitations of the other races. We cannot expect the Ogres, Trolls, or Iksar to have to struggle with our tongue on top of all the other thinking challenges they are faced with in every day life. For this reason, when speaking publicly among the other races, we cannot fill our speech with a jumble of words beyond their ken.

Following is a primer on the basics of our language. For those interested, a more advanced language guide – for when one is relaxing over wine with fellow scholars – can be found by Clicking Here.

Please remember, however, if we wish to be understood by our fellow clan members – especially the very young, or new to the clan – no more than the basics of our language should be employed.

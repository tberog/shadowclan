Description:

The Vah Shir race, is a race of cats. They make their home next to the Hollowshade Moor on the moon of Luclin. They are not considered necessarily evil but instead they begin lives more neutral, some choosing evil others good. Those that choose evil are considered beasts that use their claws and fangs to rend the flesh of their victims.

The Vah Shir can be bards, beastlords, rogues, shaman, and warriors. They do not start out with the best strength or stamina for the warrior class, nor the best wisdom for the shaman. They do, however make wonderful bards and beastlords.

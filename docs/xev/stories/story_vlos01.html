The Rogue

I was talking with another of my kind one day when he told me about a group of Ogres and Trolls which were called the shadow clan. I was amazed to hear that other dark elves lived with them. I asked myself, what possible reason would dark elves be living with Ogres and Trolls? No matter how many reasons I kept coming up with, the possibilities of making a lot of gold kept creeping into my mind.. Yes Profit! Ogres and Trolls killing and slaughtering humans, just think of the riches that would be left behind by these mindless brutes. After they killed every last human on the battlefield there would be jewels, money pouchs, weapons of all kinds scattered all over. I could be rich! I traveled for many days looking for this group called the shadow clan. Thinking such a mass mob like them would be easy to find, seeing how big they all were, I was unable to find any sign of them anywhere. On the 10th day just before the sun hit the horizon they appeared over the horizon. There must have been at least 30 of them. I didn't know what to do! I didn't dare reach for my bow with such a large group approaching me.

Unexpectingly the first one I noticed approaching me was not a Troll or an Ogre as I would have. It was a dark elf like myself, he walked very slowly towards me as a strange deep laughter came from behind him. I flexed my wrist slowly to release a hidden dagger, slowly it slid down my sleeve into my hand. The Dark elf, wearing red robes, stood before me quitely watching to see how I would react. In common tongue he turned and said only three words "He will do", those few words made me shiver like a cold gust of polar wind.

I thought I was a goner, I figured if I must die I'm going out fighting. The first to step in sight was a huge Troll, he reached down and grabbed my arm. Lifting me with ease off my feet he pulled me closer towards his face, with my dagger in hand I buried it deep within the left eye of the Troll. The Troll howled in pain and dropped to one knee. He didn't release his grip on my arm even slightly. I thought I was going to be tortured or eaten by him, but no he started to laugh a deep bellowing laugh. I stared in amazement towards the dark elf that only smirked at me. The Troll reached for the dagger with his other hand and pulled the dagger out of his eye. I stared in disbelief as his eye slowly started to heal right before my eyes.

To this very day I am not sure what the dark elf wanted of me, but my life was spared and I have been living with the shadow clan ever since. The riches are here but getting them is not as easy as I first . Trolls and Ogres are as greedy as me (smiles). I do not trust or care about the Trolls or Ogres in the least but I have grown to be somewhat friends with an odd one-eyed Troll who's eye never really healed right.

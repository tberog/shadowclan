Chug and Ratgag's First Day in Oggok

Chug being born a few days earlier had the knowledge of the area little bit before Ratgag's(Thag) arrival but only that of Oggok and Fearrot. They spent the morning searching the forest of Fearrot for Lizard Broodlings and Decaying Corpses. Many times voices could be heard throughout the forest, "Chug wer ib yu?" "Help Ratgag! dem chasing me!"

Me nub see yu, it tu dark!". The woods would never be the same again!

After a while the two of them double teaming these smaller creatures were looking for more of a challenge. They started thinking that they could clomp anything so they headed for the nearest Lizardman Camp. The plan was to surround the Lizard Mistic and clomp him first and then worry about the other two later. Well Chug layed dead next to the Mistics feet while Ratgag ran around after the mistic made him blind and accidently ran right into the water and drowned.

After about 10 minutes of sneaking up and getting there things back the two decided to see what was on the other side of the bridge. Walking accross the bridge they start seeing some large temple like buildings and then they notice one lizard warrior standing on top of one of them. Of course being the strong (head strong) Ogres that they are they attack it. Well after they finally get their things back from their corpses they decide to take the rest of the day off and take up fishing.

Sitting on the bridge with poles in hand the two of them spent the day catching many things. Heres some of the things they caught...Fish, Fish Scales, Daggers and shoes. In between spilling their beers and falling off the bridge, actually I think Ratgag pushed me, they talked about the day that they had and both had smiles on their face.

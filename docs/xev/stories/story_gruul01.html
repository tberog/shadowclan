Yug (Gruul)

The hordling

Gruul moved quietly, at least for an ogre, toward the pit. He was fascinated by the whelps, screams and howls eminating from the opening. Four other ogres, vying for the two hordling positions, lumbered along with him. Gruul could see the bloodlust and excitement in their eyes. When they neared the edge, Gruul leaned over and looked down on the bloodletting. He saw 3 dead ogres and two mammoth brutes still battling. He was surprised to see his brother still living. Nug'tug, the other ogre was the best the village had to offer and had a longstanding feud with Gruul's older brother Gruum. Gruul's hopes began to rise seeing Nug'tug trip over one of the dead ogre's outstretched hands but as Gruum stepped in to finish his opponent, Gruul cringed. He recognized the feint and braced himself as Nug'tug finished his brother off with a savage slash to the neck. Nug'tug jerked the rusty blade out of the vertebrae and bellowed a victory cry to the pair of black booted ogres watching from the rim of the pit. They grinned triumphantly and motioned him out. "Yu du gud slasha Nug," they laughed. "Yu juss git un mur an we pud yu in de horde." Tired and spent, Nug'tug flopped down on a mat of hay, content to watch the last battle, then kill his opponent for the Hordling position. The blakbuuts had come to their village to recruit 2 hordlings for the Shadowclan. Two more warriors for the collection of races that had banded together to fight against the forces of good that to pushed deeper each day into the ogre, troll and dark elf lands. The blakbuuts motioned the remaining 5 ogres into the pit. Gruul bent over his older brother's body and wiped a line of blood from the top of his head to his chin, following the ritual his shaman master had taught him. Gruul carefully thought back over his training, focusing the hate, bloodlust and love of torture into a force that could be channeled with the help of his god. Gruul was a shadowknight, a holy warrior bestowed with gifts from birth and helped along by the village shaman. No one knew of this ability but after today he understood his secret would be out. The four other ogres prepared for battle, ignoring Gruul as he was a good 200 pounds lighter and a foot shorter. He also was relatively clumsy with his scimitar. The blakbuuts tossed a lit torch into the pit and signaled the start of the last battle. Two of the ogres immediately banded together and went to work on the largest ogre in the pit. Gruul focused on his learning and effortlessly weaved through the flying blades and clubs to dish out wounds that were more annoying tremendous blow to the head, then, frustrated by being unable to get a clean hit on Gruul, went after the other lone ogre. They finished him off as well, but not before he slipped under their guard and stuck his axe a foot deep into the side of one of them. Gruul, in a state of meditation and focused on his god, could see the lifeforce leaking out of the ogre. He stepped closer and with an open hand, sucked the remaining essence from the ogre's body. The combatant collapsed at the feet of its uncomprehending ally. By the time its partner looked away Gruul had slashed it across the forearm, severing tendons and ligaments causing it to drop its weapon. The ogre looked down at the club and then back up to the scimitar jutting out from its belly. Gruul jerked it left then up, allowing the artery to spurt blood and gore on his chest and face. He lifted his foot, placed it next to the entry wound, and pushed the ogre's body off his blade. A dark and confused look passed over the blakbuuts' faces and they huddled in discussion. An hour and two more dead ogres later, they were on the road to the clan encampment, two brand new hordlings in tow, Nug'tug and Gruul. The blakbuuts walked next to Nug'tug, keeping a good distance between them and Gruul. The faceless one had infused his student with great understanding of his powers and instilled a profound hatred toward the simple warriors. They nervously continued on, all but Nug'tug casting strained glances back at the future Shadowknight.

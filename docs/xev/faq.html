The F.A.Q.
(Frequently Asked Questions)

Within these pages lies knowledge passed down through the clan. Hopefully you will find some of it useful. If by chance you believe that you might have knowledge trapped away in your brainpan that others might seek please discuss it further with Xaya.

Q. What should I fight if I'm blah level?
A. You need to look for monsters that are your level or lower. To do this you need to target them, and hit enter to go to "chat" mode/ Then type command [/consider], that will tell you what approximate level a monster is. At first level all monsters should be a "Even fight" to text will be white. As you progress in levels the monsters "cons" will change color. At first level you will have three color "cons". White (even fight), Yellow (Gamble), and Red (Tombstone).

Q. How do I setup up autorun?
A. To do this go to small window mode by hitting F1. Then in the lower right hand corner, you will see a button labeled RUN. Click it once. Now you are running.

Q. How to I put things in my hotkey list?
A. This one is a little tricky; you need to find the button you want to make a hot key. Let's say you want your spells on a hotkey so you can meditate fast. Go to small window again, and look for the button that is labeled spells. Place your cursor over the spells button and left click and hold down the mouse button. After a few seconds, a box called spells will appear in your cursor. Take the upper left hand corner of it and move it to the bunch of boxes in the bottom left hand part of your screen. Put the corner of it in the first box. Click - now you have your spells labeled as the number 1. To test this, hit the 1 key and you should go to your spellbook.

Q. How can I kill a skeleton? They seem too hard?
A. There are many kinds of skeletons. If you're first starting out, look for decaying skeletons. All the others will kill you until about 5th level or so.

Q. How do I fish?
A. You need a fishing pole. You also need some bait. Then you need a body of water. Go to your actions box and look for fishing. Under skills. All races have the skill. Equip the fishing pole, and then get by a body of water. Click the fishing button. Hoowah!! Now your fishing!.

Q. I keep drowning when I jump in the water...why?
A. You need to look up when swimming..I use mouse look to do this. Refer to your manual to see how to mouse look. I use the mouse2 button for mouse look. Just look up and move like normal. You can now swim to your hearts content.

Q. How can I tell if something is too tough?
A. Refer to the question about what can I fight

Q. How do I join or make a group?
A. To join a group, you need somebody to group with. I will assume you already have this. Let's have you invite them into your group. First target them, and then go to small window mode. Then look for the button that is labeled "INVITE", then click it. You just sent them an invite to join a group with you. You can keep adding people to your group, up to 6 people can be in a group at one time. Now to let you get added in a group when you get an invite. Remember that label for invite? Well, now its called "JOIN". When you see in the chat box "So-and-so has invited you to join his/her party", go back to small window and that label that was for invite, is now called Join. Click it and you're in their group.

Q. How do you make group talk?
A. Once again, go to small window mode. Then click on where your chat box is. You will see some tabs for all kinds of chat filters. One of them is for group speak. Click the box and now you're in group speak.

Q. What should I use my starting training points on if I am a blah?
A. This is kind of up to you. But it helps to put your starting points into your weapon skill. You might at later levels start saving your points for some trade skill. But that's all up to you.

Q. When I am fighting in a group with higher level people it seems they keep getting more experience points. Is this true and if so, why?
A. They are getting more exp. then you. But you get the same hard movement on your exp. bar that the highest level person gets. So if you kill a tombstone monster and the highest level person gets his exp. bar moved by 1/5th of a bar..You will also move 1/5th of a bar.

Q. What's a "faction"?
A. A faction is how a certain race feels about your race/class. For example, as a dark elf cleric, I am allowed to trade with the human merchants that dwell in East commons, but if I was a dark elf Necromancer, I would be KOS (Kill On Sight) to them.

Q. Do factions matter? (remember Troll SKs are KOS in the Ogre Warrior training area)
A. Oh yes they do! I have a story for you. There was a person in P3 that was a dark elf. This person thought it would be a good idea to go help some humans kill the guards that wander the forest in Neriak.. After a while of doing this, and making some good money from the loot of the guards, he walked back to town. Little did he know that now he was unwelcome in Neriak. All guards would attack him on sight. Killing some monsters will raise your faction in one place. While lowering it to others. For example, if I am a dark elf Necromancer, and I go to east commons and kill orcs, after a while I will be able to go into the city of Freeport

Q. Does intelligence help me advance quicker?
A. It helps your skills advance faster. A dark elf warrior will hit the level/skill cap faster then a Brute will. This allows the Dark elf to be able to keep several weapon skill up to max. So he could use both two handed slash (Two-handed sword), and One hand slash (long sword).

Q. What does that "bash" skill do?
A. Bash for ogres/trolls is a free attack they get in addition to their normal attack. It can also at higher skill stun a monster for a few seconds.

Q. How do I control my pet?
A. There are several commands you can give your pet.

/Pet follow
/Pet sit down (this puts your pet in a relaxed state and he wont wander around. He will still get up if you are hit)
/Pet kill (go get him pet!!)
/Pet back off (commands your pet to back out of combat. If not given a pet will follow a fleeing monster. Not good in you are in a dungeon and the monster gets some friends.
More can be found in your manual.

So You want to Join Shadowclan?

We are always looking for like-minded players to join our ranks. Below are a few items that you should know will be expected of any member:

  1. First and foremost, Shadowclan is a Roleplaying Guild... we act like what we choose to be in Norrath For example:, if you choose to play an ogre, then you are expected to act and speak like an ogre (dumb and brutish). This is the foundation of our guild and what we try to do best.
  2. You should make every effort to frequent the Shadowclan Website and messageboards. Here you will find news on changes to the clan, events, gatherings, sales, etc. You should feel free to speak up and post as well as read messages.

Once you have finished registering............

There will be an Email reply to your registration, and then you will need to contact your races Leaders to be added to the roster. You will be added to the guild on a trial basis for 2 weeks. If during these 2 weeks you group with Shadowclan members and prove yourself as being a Roleplayer and a good addition then your membership will be Final.

Go register on the messageboards and read through the various different forums. Under the Scrubdom Forum, new recruits are requried to post a thread with their character name & race as the subject heading. Then they will write a short blurb about their character either in role-play fashion (preferably) or not - doesn't matter at this point - this will promote use of the boards clan communication purposes. NOTE: This Posting on the Scrubdom Boards is REQUIRED to get permanent status in Shadowclan.

During this Trial period you should be making new friends within the guild and proving yourself and your roleplay ability. And again, visit the webpage and messageboards frequently to stay current on events and happenings.

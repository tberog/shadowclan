Clan Race Guides

Shadowclan is composed of various evil races. Each has their own unique customs and mannerisms. Below is a small collection of scrolls which helps to teach about the different races and the professions which they practice. It is hoped that by reading these scrolls one might have a better understanding of how they wish to live their lives in norrath and in Shadowclan.

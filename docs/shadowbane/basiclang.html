Basic Language – Advanced language

Four words to survive

Yes/Okay -> Ki
No/Not -> Jov
Hello -> Jha
Farewell -> Jha'ov "-ov" makes things mean the opposite

Pronouns

Me/Myself/I/My -> Nu
We/Our -> Nha
You/Your/Yourself/You all/Yourselfs -> Lak
He/She/It/Her/His/Its/Himself/Herself/Itself/Them/Their/Themselves -> Dek

Basic Verbs

Is/Am/Are -> Kham
Know/Understand -> Gruk "Nu Gruk'ov" means "I do not understand"
Talk/Speak/Tell -> Tahk
Go -> Gu
Follow -> Arha
Hunt -> Sekr
Attack -> Jalla "Jall" is the irekei word for a battle
Sleep/Logout -> Bollo

Common words

and -> aht
or -> ur
but -> bhut
to -> tu
for -> fhor
how -> hou

wh- -> uh-
who -> uho
what -> uhat
where -> uhere
why -> uhy
when -> uhen

th- -> qh-
the -> qhe
then -> qhen
this -> qhis
that -> qhat

Common exclamations

Hoorah! -> Hkuul!
#$&%@*! -> Skah!
It is the Dragon's will -> Nkoth

Combat terms

stamina -> qir also is the irekei word for "breath" or "air"
health -> khan also is the irekei word for "blood"
spirit/mana/magic -> krys
pull, bait, lure -> lura
help, aid, assist -> asit
summon, call to you -> kal
scout, detect, search -> sirch
stealth, sneak, hide -> haid
heal me -> khan nu also can do /em bleeds
experience -> lirnan
rank -> skar
non-Irekei -> firk short version of Fir'khanim, which literally means rain bleeders
Dragon -> Nkur short version of Nkurakarin, Terror of Terrors

Other terms

money/coin -> bhon irekei word for "bone", which at one point were used for commerce
big/good/powerful -> iri irekei word for "hot"
small/bad/weak -> uri irekei word for "cold"
thanks -> Bho irekei word for "beautiful"

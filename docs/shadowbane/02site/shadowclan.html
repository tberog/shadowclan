SHADOWCLAN IN SHADOWBANE PROPOSAL
We are Shadowclan. So named for our willingness to continue our fight in the shadows and even when the Blessed Sun is hidden. Though the Sun is the source of our strength, when Her eternal light and heat are taken from us by the machinations of the Meddler God, we continue on with our purpose, undaunted and even more resolved. The Burning Sands are no place for fir'khanim, be it day or night. When we stand tall upon the Anvil of the Sun, the shadow of our power shall be seen across the lands.
Guild Type:
Irekei-only. Our base guild will be a Virakt with possible specialty sub-guilds being formed/added later.
Purpose:
To promote roleplaying in the online gaming community and to add flavor and an immersive environment in the hopes that we, and those who interact with us will have a more enjoyable and interesting gaming experience.
Playstyle:
Exclusively RP, with heavy amounts of PvP action. Antagonistic, ruthless, zealous, self-serving, xenophobic and haughty.

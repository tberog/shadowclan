Creating a Shadowclan Character in Shadowbane

Anyone who wishes to join Shadowclan, must be born into the Clan. IE, new characters are REQUIRED. There are many reasons for this, the main one being the fact that 'growing' your character within the Clan is the best way to promote bonding and provide a sense of family and community. There are no exceptions to this rule.

IMPORTANT NOTE: This is all based on various info released about the game so far. More than likely, things will be quite different by the time the game is actaully released. This is for general ideas only, and should not be considered to be the final word or even correct at this point.

 

Creating an Irekei for Shadowbane

 

Irekei Starting Attributes:

Strength: 40 (Max: 90) Patron: The Dragon, Terror of Terrors
Dexterity: 55 (Max: 130)
Constitution: 40 (Max: 90) Height: 6'5" - 7'5" (Male)
Intelligence: 45 (Max: 115) Height: 6'1" - 7'1" (Female)
Spirit: 30 (Max: 85)
    Strength: Affects melee damage, and encumbrance, and certain equipment may have minimum strength requirements to equip.

    Dexterity: Affects various roguish skills, melee and ranged to-hit, and provides a defense bonus to lightly-armored characters.

    Constitution: Used to calculate Health and Stamina, resistances to poison and disease, and your character's visual bulkiness.

    Intelligence: Has a direct effect on skill levels, although it is not known in what manner; it may be that the number of skill points received at each level-up is determined by your intelligence, or it may simply be that high intelligence provides a percentage bonus to all skill checks. May also be used to calculate mana for mage-types, although it's possible that Spirit is used for all mana calaculations.

    Spirit: Mana stat for all Healer-based classes, and possibly for mages as well. May also play into magic resistance.

Talents and Traits:

Irekei begin character creation with 10 points unassigned. These can be used to augment starting attributes, or can be used to purchase talents and traits. You may also lower some of your starting attributes in order to purchase additional talents or traits, or to raise other starting attributes or give skill bonuses. It is not known at this time whether Irekei will have access to all talents and traits

Other talents: Other merits can be purchased during character creation as well; such options as Night Vision and Ambidexterity were seen in the charactter-creation screenshots. Some or all of these may be rolled into racial packages, and not purchasable separately; we'll have to wait til the game is closer to release to see the full list. Note that no more than 5 talents or traits of any sort may be purchased by a character.

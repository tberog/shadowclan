Joining Shadowclan in Shadowbane

At the moment, since the game hasn't started, there is really no official way to join the Clan. However, you are encouraged to participate on our forums at http://www.shadowclan.cx. Once the game actually begins, Shadowclan has an open membership policy, accepting anyone willing to follow our rules and our character creation guidelines. No application process is involved. You simply make your character, join us at our home and begin the intake process in game as your character.

Anyone who wishes to join Shadowclan, must be born into the Clan. IE, new characters are REQUIRED. There are many reasons for this, the main one being the fact that 'growing' your character within the Clan is the best way to promote bonding and provide a sense of family and community. There are no exceptions to this rule.

Before creating a character to join Shadowclan, please review this website and keep in mind the following:

    Shadowclan is IREKEI ONLY. Potential members should be prepared to limit their character's interactions with non-Irekei.

    There may be physical requirements for character creation also, but any details are yet to be determined.

    There are no proposed limitations for class choice at this time, though this may be subject to change as Beta progresses and we have a chance to get a better feel for the game.

    ROLEPLAY (see the Ways of the Clan section). Stay in-character - No references to the world outside of Shadowbane. Use of the Irekei Language is strongly encouraged. (Note: If your character NAME is inapporopriate you will NOT be allowed to join Shadowclan).

    HAVE FUN without ruining the fun of others.

    Come prepared to LEARN. Expect at the very least a 30 day training period before you are allowed full membership status in Shadowclan. We have high expectations that members must achieve and maintain.

    Come prepared to SHARE AND SACRAFICE. Shadowbane is a community where each individual is expected to work for the improvement of that community rather than individual gain. Lower ranked members are often restricted in the types of equipment they may use and everyone is restricted as far as personal wealth is concerned.

    NO EXPLOITS. Shadowclan members do not abuse bugs, cheats, external programs or ways to gain power or wealth that the developers of the game did not intend to be used.

    LOYALTY. Shadowclan (and Irekei in particular) are fiercely loyal to their group (Virakt.

Rules for Shadowclan

Chain of Command:

All characters are expected to follow the chain of command.

Looting:

Looting proceeds according to the chain of command. The highest Clan member gets first choice of loot from all monster and player corpses. Lower-ranked members should not even touch any corpses until all of those ranked above them have already picked over the loot or the higher-ranked members have given them special permission to do so for that instance. On a related note, higher-ranked members are expected to help out the lower-ranked members as needed with new equipment and/or repairs. They are also allowed to distribute looted equipment to lower-ranked members. Non-Clan members that are in the area or along on the trip should never touch any corpses unless specifically told to do so by the highest ranking Clan member present.

Out-of-Game Behaviour:

All Shadowclan members are expected to be mature in actions if not years. Any immature behaviour by a Shadowclan Player reflects badly on the guild as a whole, especially if done under the Shadowclan name or the character's name.

The Shadowclan Irekei Language

Much of the Irekei culture centers around the things they view as being most important, mainly the sun, desert and the Dragon. Many words, terms and phrases reflect their veneration of these objects.

Essentially, Irekei language is a series of root words combined with suffixes or prefixes to create compound words or complex thoughts. One of the most commonly known words to outsider is fir'khan, used in reference to non-Irekei. . Many words will often have duplicate meanings for various concepts concepts related to their beliefs. One example would be the word troni, meaning strong. The root word, tro, is a verb meaning to rise. Given the Irekei veneration of the sun, they have taken it a step further and use troni, which is the active tense, and equated it with the concept for strength.

Irekei means "They who Endure" or "they who will survive."

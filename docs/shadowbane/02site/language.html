The Irekei Language

This page is mostly for roleplay and immersiveness. Shadowclan characters aren't expected to know the complete Shadowclan Irekei language. Some may choose to learn more, some less. However, every Clan memebr is expected to have a decent knowledge of the Basic Words and Terms. Anything further is up to you. Words with an asterick (*) next to them should always be used rather than translated. For those who wish to delve deeply into the Shadowclan Irekei language, there is a detailed Shadowclan Irekei Language page that deals with language root extrapolations, grammar, sentence structure and contains a much more detailed list of words.

Irekei means "They who Endure" or "they who will survive."

BASIC WORD AND TERMS:

Fir'khanim - non-Irekei (literally "rain bleeder," a derogatory term for any being weak enough to waste water through sweat)
* Virakt - Tribe; Clan
* Khar'ika - the flame of Khalikryst, the holy fire that transformed the Irekei from Elves into superior beings, and the reserve of strength and endurance that an Irekei calls upon to survive at all costs. Battle and tests of fortitude can kindle an Irekei's khar'ika, making it burn brighter, while death snuffs out the khar'ika forever
Jov'uus - slaves

Kryquo’khalin - The Dragon; "Holy Source of the Sun"
Khalikryst - Goddess of the Sun; The Transforming Fire; "Holy Fire"
KryKhalin - the Blessed Sun (reverent term)

Ki - Yes; affirmation
Jov - No; negation
Jah - Greeting/Hello

Nkoth - statement meaning "It is The Will of the Dragon"

* Vrek - outcast
Skath - Irekei name for the scouring Desert Wind which is said to be so fearsome in the depths of the desert that it can flay skin from bone
 Skah - exclamation of contempt
Hkuul, Huuvah - exclamation of joy; battle cry
Zult - bladed weapon
Kigg - spear
Gruk - understand

Greenlands - non-desert terrain
Whitelands - the Frozen North; snow-covered terrain
Sun - a measure of time; equivalent to one day
Hateful Ones - Elves
Cold Ones - can be used to refer to any fir'khanim but is usually used in reference to Shades
Living Stones - Dwarves
Dragon Kin - Drakes
Meddler God - All-Father

"You are as soft as the Greenlands!"
-Irekei insult

TITLES:

L’alar Khalinka - Sun Dancer; "One whose spirit is of the Sun"
Khanarch’alar - Blood Prophet; Chosen of the Dragon

Urikhan - (Cold Blooded One; pre Rite of Passage)
Irikhan - (Hot Blooded One; post Rite of Passage)
Sek'alar - (Hunter; warrior of note)
Khal'usht - (Flame Keeper; typically older Irekei who advise leaders)
Arkhalar - (Blade Wielder; literally "clawed one", a Family head)
Khal'urvho - (Phoenix; literally "Fiery bird")
Khar'uus - (Blood Lord; literally "Fire Master")
Kryqhi'khalin - (Chosen One; literally "Blessed Spawn of the Sun")

EXTENDED WORD LIST:

Jallakar = war

Khan'jallakar - Blood War
Krykhan’jallakar - apocalypse; the final Blood War; "Holy Blood War"

PHRASES:

Kha - formal greeting; short for 'May your khar'ika shine brightly'
Nu'Jall - literally "My fight"; a signal for others to stand down; usually used when one-on-one combat is desired
Tu'ghoko - a threat; literally "You die"
Tu'uri - an insult; literally, "You cold"; generally used to sneer at fir'khanim or to provoke a fight with another Irekei

PREFIXES AND SUFFIXES:

jov-   negation
khal-   fiery; flaming; of or related to the Sun
khar-  fire / blood; unique Irekei concept usually used in reference to their Inner Blood Fire
kry-   blessed; chosen; holy
nu-   I; self; possesive modifier
tu-   you; singular; possesive modifier
vas-  great; big; large (as in size)

-al   formal plural
-im   informal plural

-akar   great; mighty (as in strength)
-akt   collective
-alar   one (in reference to a person); one who
-ika   of the soul
-in  of
-kryst   mystical; holy
-qhi   spawn
-quo   source
-uus   master; lord
-ukt   member of a clan or tribe
-ulan   one who makes/creates
-ular   one who does/uses

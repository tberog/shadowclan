Origins of the Dragon

As told by Vas'khanim

In the time before, there was nothing but The Void and the stars. Great beings of incomprehensible power traversed the vast spaces, devouring the energy of the stars and fighting amongst themselves for supremacy. The greatest among these was one who was nameless but would later be known as, Kryquo’khalin, the Terror of Terrors. For unmeasureable aeons, these beings fought, only to be vanquished; either destroyed or chased to the unknown depths of The Void. Victorious over all of the lesser ones, the Dragon, alone, gorged upon the energy of stars too numerous to count. Satiated, the Dragon slept, curled up for an eternity. The sleeping giant dreamt, and from its dreams sprang the very world we live upon. The fire from the Dragon warmed the earth and many species and manners of beings appeared, though only one would later be chosen as worthy of the Dragon.

There the Dragon slumbered for untold millenia while the peoples upon its back prospered and grew, unknowing of their true origins. Attracted to the energy and heat radiating from the world, some of the Dragon's foes that had escaped its grasp in the Before-time came to this place and used their powers to meddle with its peoples. Annoyed at such audacity, the Dragon stirred in its sleep, and eventually awoke, confronting these Meddlers in the Great Battle. Long was the battle fought, and much was destroyed before the Dragon returned to slumber. The foes rejoiced, believing themselves victorious.But, in their ignorance, they were unaware that the true goal of the Dragon's waking had already been accomplished.

For Kryquo’khalin, infinitely wise in foresight, had used its power to transform one of these lesser beings into Khalikryst, Phoenix Goddess, the Transforming Fire, dread Lady of the Sun, she who would eventually transform us in-turn, so that we coul realize our true purpose and serve the Dragon as best we are able.

But that tale is best left to another time.

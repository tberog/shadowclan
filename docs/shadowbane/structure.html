The content on this site represents the current state of Shadowclan thinking about its participation in Shadowbane. Many things are in a state of flux during the beta phase of this game's development including our thinking on the way we'll play Irekei.

We have an ongoing discussion on many questions around Irekei & Shadowclan roleplay on our forums. Please visit us there.


Structure

During this stage of beta our structure is a loose one. An Elder or Inner Council led by Azog directs and guides our activities. Members of the Clan Helm are also actively involved in organizing things. And, just as importantly, we look to all our testers for feedback, suggestions and help.

Discussion of the clan's structure and the criteria for membership in the clan are active questions on our private forum. Shadowclan members who are active in beta can arrange access to those forums by contacting Khan.

Overview

The Shadowclan Orks are a division of Big Head Uzrok Deathfist's trusted Iron Orks. We believe firmly in Orkish superiority and dominance, and agressively pursue establishing it. Unlike the short-sighted Dragonbound however, we believe as Uzrok that utter destruction of the world is not in our best interest. Extorting resources out of the lesser races brings us as much pleasure as burning their homes to the ground, and we've been known to allow our prey to leave with their lives after paying tribute to our dominance. Blackmailing other nations with the threat of siege, or even capturing an enemy settlement only to sell it back to them at extortionist rates are among the many tactics employed by the Shadowclan in the name of Uzrok.

While we operate as a self-maintained unit for the most part, Uzrok will from time to time send word of a special assignment he can trust only the Shadowclan to carry out, and we do so with unshakable fervor. Between assignments, our own Generals organize hunting parties and raids all over Agon, while our crafters industriously ready the tools of war. Though our task of pillaging the world may require relocation from city to city on occasion, sometimes even basing our campaign out of a city owned by like-minded Orks, there is always the thought that we may one day find a place to establish a new Ork Capital, far from the influence of the Dragonbound in Flaming Skull, where Uzrok may rally further support and one day return to remove the meddlesome Dragonbound and their scheming Red Dragon Arxanthos from Morak once and for all.

What Shadowclan is About

  • Open Membership - Anyone can make a properly named Ork and join Shadowclan.
  • Role play - We are always in-character while in-game.
  • Xenophobic - We avoid interaction with non-Orks unless it serves our purpose. Use of trusted Mahirim scouts is not however uncommon.
  • Making the game more fun - We have fun and increase the fun for others too.
  • Respect - We respect other players as fellow players.
  • Quality - We train our new members in the Shadowclan ways and our expectations are enforced.
  • Commitment - In order to stay in Shadowclan after an initial trial period, members must permanently commit their character to Shadowclan.
  • Community - Shadowclan members work together and share supplies freely among themselves.
  • Game Friendly - Our storyline and background blend in with game's storyline and background to the highest degree possible. We won't force other players to break character in order to match our own role play style. We follow the rules of the game. No cheating, bug exploiting, illegal 3rd party programs, or other violations of the game's Terms of Service are ever allowed.

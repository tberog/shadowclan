Basic Pict Language

This is a basic list of the pictish language. We borrowed alot from the Irekei language with some changes thrown in that we either made up or borrowed from old Aztec. This document will change a bit as we begin to use the language and work out the kinks. Additions will be made once we make some decisions on words that have yet to make it on this list. Big thanks to Khasha'an for helping myself and the Warchiefs in putting this together.

Word List

Hello - "Da Mat"
Farewell - "Da'et" ("et" makes things negative)
Yes - "Eo"
No - "Ket"

Pronouns, four simple words to refer to yourself and others

Me - "Ne" (Also used for Myself, I and My) (though most Pict tend to speak in third person)
We - "Nha" (Also Our)
You - "Mat" (Also Your,Yourself,You all, and Yourselves)
It - "Dek" (Also Him, Her, He, She, Himself, Herself, Them, Their etc)

Common verbs, when following orders one must know what the order is

Know - "Grok" ("Ne Grok'et" means "I do not understand")
Am - "Kham" (Also Is, Be, and Are)
Talk - "Tahk" (Also Speak and Tell)
Follow - "Arha"
Hunt - "Sekr"
Attack - "Jalla"
Sleep/Logout - "Bollo"
Go - Gho

Common exclamations, cheers and jeers

Hoorah! - "Hkuul" or "Hukhaa"
#$&%@*! - "Skah" or "Garm"( curse words)
Whoa! or Yikes! - Gah!
Common combat terms
health - "qhan" (pictish word for "blood")
stamina - "qir" (pictish word for "breath" or "air")
mana - "qrys" (pictish word for magic)
non-pict - "dog"
experience - "learning"
rank - Age, or having much wisdom of a Pict being many levels above you.
Other terms
money - any amount of money is usually refered to as a "handful ov tins"
good - "iri" (Also Big or Powerful.)
bad - "uri" (Also Small or Weak.)
thanks - "bho"
Common words
and - "aht"
Because of/As a result of - Ibh
But - Uhb
For - Iko
From - Ik
How - Zihn
Of - Ov
Or - Uht
Much - Cho
Little - Chi
Very - Hru
Nothing - Xo
Words that begin with the letters “TH” should be replaced with “QH”
Words that begin with the letters “WH” should be replaced with “UH”

wh- -> uh-

Examples
who - uho
what - uhat
where - uhere
why - uhy
when - uhen

th- -> qh-

Examples
the - qhe
then - qhen
this - qhis
that - qhat

Uhere kham mat? Qhere mat kham!

Numbers
0 - zekh
1 - onkh
2 - tukh
3 - trkh
4 - fokh
5 - fikh
6 - sikh
7 - sekh
8 - igkh
9 - nikh
10 - onkh'zekh
11 - onkh'onkh
etc

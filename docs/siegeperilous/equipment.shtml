EQUIPMENT

All orcs may use this armor:
Leather, Studded Leather, Ringmail, Orc Helm. Bone armor is allowed except bone tunics and bone helms.

All non-mojoka may use these shields:
Wooden, Brown kite, metal round, buckler.

All non-mojoka may use these weapons:
All 2-handed axes, mace, maul, club, war mace, war hammer, long spear, quarter staff, short spear, kryss, war fork, cutlass, scimitar, bow, crossbow, heavy, crossbow, bardiche. All little weapons (knives, daggers, hammers).

All new Age of Shadows weapons are allowed except for the Lance, Scythe and Bone harvester (Bone harvester is usable only by Magruka).

Mojoka may use only: gnarled staff, quarter staff, black staff, wands, scrolls, robes, daggers, knives

Magruka may use only: dagger, bone harvester, scythe, scepter.

The following are never allowed to be used by any orc: most swords, hammer pick, waraxe, halberd, katana, lance, scythe, heater shield, bronze shield, metal kite shield, chainmail, platemail, bone tunic/helm, leather cap, "Humie" clothes, and disguise kits.

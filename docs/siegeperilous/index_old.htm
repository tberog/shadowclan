About Shadowclan On Siege Perilous

Shadowclan on Ultima Online Siege Perilous is about roleplaying orcs and having fun while not ruining the fun of others. We roleplay orcs that are often hostile to anyone invading our lands. If you are looking for a fun community oriented guild with emphasis on roleplay and group cooperative PvP, then look no further. If you are a humie, looking to interact with us, expect to battle with us unless you manage to offer cider and food to distract us long enough to get away. Any human in the orc fort will be attacked.

For those familiar with Shadowclan on Catskills, please note that Shadowclan Siege Perilous has different rules and structure. Read up on the Shadowclan "Clan Rulz" (click button on the right) and note that we are much more aggressive on this shard. If you encounter problems with members of Shadowclan on Siege Perilous, click on "Problemz" to the left.

Clan Structure

The basic clan chain of command is :
Rukh grunts > Rukh-less grunts > Gruntees


New members start out as gruntees and are promoted to grunt once they have learned the rules and passed the clan's grunt training process. Gruntees must follow the orders of grunts.

Any large group of grunts can form a Rukh (large self-organizing armies of about 25 orcs; actual number required to form Rukh depends on current number of Rukh). These Rukh are simply a chain of command for the members of the Rukh. Any grunt can join or quit a Ruhk, though a Rukh-less grunt is lower ranked than any Rukh grunt. A Rukh can not restrict membership or set any restrictions beyond the general shadowclan rules, but it can place certain members at the bottom of the rukh chain of command. A Rukh requires about 25 grunts initially to form, and is disbanded if active membership drops below 20 (actual numbers depend on the current number of Rukhs in the clan).

Next Page

Rukhs, while allowed to have disputes with each other, are not allowed to all-out war with each other. Rukhs are encouraged (but not required) to cooperate by setting up cross rukh leadership based on mutual consent. Any orc granted clan wide authority by ALL the rukh leaders can wear a cape. Rukh leaders also have sashes at their disposal to indicate rukh ranks in any manner they chose.

Tribes are self-organizing small to large groups of grunts (at least 2 grunts required to form a tribe), often based on a particular roleplay style, and often having their own internal chain of command. Tribes can exist in a rukh or outside a rukh, but not across rukh (due to chain of command conflicts). Tribal authority is entirely self-contained. Unlike a Rukh, Tribes can place restrictions (for example, addtional equipment limitations) on its members. It is common for a Rukh to have several tribes.

The organization of Siege Perilous Shadowclan is designed to simply provide the framework and basic guidelines. It is up to the members of the Clan to form the Rukhs and Tribes and make changes to them as necessary. The structure opens opportunity for leadership to any member who earns the respect of fellow members.

Clan Rulz

Looting : Take what you can immediately use. Orcish weapons and armor, gold, etc. Should also take away victim's fighting capability (weapons, arrows, reagents) if they are a threat to continue the fight. If on toll trip, just take toll. If player was verbally abusive, take a bit more. Use good judgement. Don't ruin the fun of others. Never trash another player's equipment. Keep in mind this is a PvP shard designed to be harsh. Higher ranked orcs have priority on looting. Only Shadowclan orcs can loot on Shadowclan territory and orc lead trips. As for looting other Shadowclan orcs - use your head and be aware of the potential consequences. If (and only if) you are a higher rank - take what you need or want. Everyone of every rank should pick up and hold things for your fellow orcs if you can. Remember we are a community and can't function if we cripple our own members.
Murder count : Orcs are required to be "red murderer" at all times. (Rare exceptions granted only by specific approval of clan leadership). This means you must have at least 5 murder counts at all times (you can easily get these by killing NPC vendors, such as the farmers near Skara Brae.)

Clan Rulz

Thingies : All items of any kind in an orc's possession technically belong to the Shadowclan community. Orcs are NOT allowed to use banks. Orcs are NOT allowed to be friended or co-ownered to any building. Only exception to the house rule are buildings maintained by Shadowclan. All grunts will have access to one building for shared storage purposes. Orcs are NOT allowed to have boats unless they have permission.
Other : Stay In Character at all times while in-game. Follow the equipment guidelines and the chain of command. Do not call anyone Dewds or Pks. They are all "pushdug humies" (stinky humans). Do not be judgemental regarding the playstyle of other players. Do not whine about how other players play the game (in-game or out of game). We chose to come here and deal with the challenges of this unique shard. If a problem can't be handled in-character, handle it out of game. Show respect for each other, and other players not in Shadowclan.

Shadowclan History

Shadowclan began on Siege Perilous as a chaotic band of gruntees. Over time, more and more orcs advanced to the rank of grunt, bringing some stability and order to the clan. Eventually twenty five grunts from three different tribes (the skull krackers, battle surgins, and blakfists) came together and formed the Hukor Rukh, an army deticated to the legendary warrior orc Hukor. The first warboss of this Rukh was the Blakfist Parfug, and he led the clan to many a great victory over the humans.

As the clan grew in size, the Krath'Kuul Rukh emerged. Founded by the aged mojoka K'Dahbruh, the Krath'Kuul Rukh literally means "Army of Servants to the Gods." Two tribes initially populated the Krath'Kuuls, the Followers of the Buurz'Ash and the Kyn'Tahj Tribe.

The two Rukhs united first under the Warlord Parfug.

Over the years, many Warlords, tribes and even Rukhs have come and gone. What happens in the future is in the hand of the orcs themselves.

Problems

If you have a problem with a member of Shadowclan, report it to Quality Control (QC).
To do this, ICQ 23063396.
QC's job is to deal with orcs that are:
  • Harrassing
  • OOC Behavior
  • OOG actions that hurt clan
  • Traitors
  • Over looting
  • Failing to follow clan rules
QC will handle problems by:
  • Warning and educating the player
  • Demoting player to gruntee
  • Kicking player out of clan

Quality control is important in making sure Shadowclan stays true to its guiding prinicple of not ruining the fun of others.

EQUIPMENT


All orcs may use this armor:
Leather, Studded Leather, Ringmail, Orc Helm. Bone armor is allowed except bone tunics and bone helms.

All non-mojoka may use these shields:
Wooden, Brown kite, metal round, buckler.

All non-mojoka may use these weapons:
All 2-handed axes, mace, maul, club, war mace, war hammer, long spear, short spear, kryss, war fork, cutlass, scimitar, bow, crossbow, heavy, crossbow, bardiche. All little weapons (knives, daggers, hammers).

Making an Orc

Remember, only one character is allowed per account on Siege Perilous. So do it right the first time.

Select "Advanced" character creation. Select 40 Strength, 15 Dexterity, and 10 Intelligence. Pick 50 hiding (must be able to hide to join). As an orc, you can use any skill EXCEPT magery (only Mojoka can use magery). There are no mojoka spots available at this time. When spots open up, it will be announced on the message board).

Choose an Orcish Name. Click here for some example names. Note that your name must not be based on humie words, it must have proper capitalization, and must not be in use by another member. Add apostrophes to help make your name unique and do a name search here to make sure you aren't using someone else's name. Then you may want to click on "Get Help" to verify that your name is acceptable.

More Instructions
Making an Orc

Select NO hair, and NO facial hair. Orcish light green skin is strongly preferred (2 from the right, 3 from the top is a pretty good color). You can be a male or a female orc.

Alternatively, start in Skara Brae and get your murder counts right away by killing the farmers on the mainland, so that you can get on the clan guildstone right away.

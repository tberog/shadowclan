Language

Background

The language is based largely off Huttese

Required Words

Hi = Chut (Chut Chut)
Goodbye = Meku (short version of Mee jewz ku)
yes = yeah
no = noah
okay = okey
great/good/big = bubhosha
not good/small = nubhosha
Hoowah! = Hoowah!
$%#@ = skah
understand/know = gruka

me/I/my = Mee
we = Meemee
you = uba
them = huba

Fun huttese

stupid/fool = stoopa

Converting English to Shadowclan Rodian

Just add vowels to the end of words, especially "a" and "o".

Note - the phrases "Thank you" and "please" does not exist in huttese or Shadowclan vocabulary. No self-respecting Rodian should use them.

Examples

You! Fire your blaster to kill the stupid wookie.
Uba! Shooto uba blasto toh crispo tha stoopa wooky

Darn, its time for me to sleep (log out).
Skah, itsa tee-toky foro mee toa winkee.

How to Join
Why Shadowclan?

Fight for a cause! Shadowclan provides a context, something worth fighting for. It does this through an immersive roleplaying experience, a large membership community, communal sharing of equipment and supplies, members committed to helping each other out, a strong desire for group combat, and a mature membership dedicated to having fun while not ruining the fun of others.

We are open membership, so joining is easy. Try out Shadowclan - hundreds have and hundreds have loved it!

What you need to do:

1. Read about our language. Most find it helpful to print it out.

2. Read about what we expect. You are expected to know it.

3. Make a rodian with a proper rodian name on the Kettemoor galaxy. You may be asked to create a new character if your name is deemed inappropriate. It is always good to check on the message boards if a name is appropriate.

4. Finish the tutorial, and take the shuttle to Mos Eisley on the planet Tattoine.

5. Contact a shadowclan member. They can refer you to someone who has powers to invite you to the PA. If any are available, they will get in touch with you

6. You will be asked some questions to ensure you know what is expected of you, and that you know some basic words in the language.

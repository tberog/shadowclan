What we expect:

In-character

Members must be in-character at all times. The only exception is /tell which can be OOC, and only out of necessity. Being in-character is easy and has two parts - language and behavior.

Language

Read about our language. Most find it helpful to print it out. Use it when interacting with other players.

Behavior

Be helpful to fellow clan members. Be neutral to non-clan rodians, not all are the peace loving traitors. Some may support the grand protector and be willing to fight and support the clan. We despise all non-rodians, they neither understand nor appreciate the thrill of the hunt.

Though we prefer to roleplay hostility, we do not condone player harassment of any kind. Refer to the rules of conduct.

Related to behavior is also the matter of exploits and loyalty.

No exploits

If a member is shown to use exploits, and does not cease such behavior when confronted, they will be removed from the clan and deemed a traitor.

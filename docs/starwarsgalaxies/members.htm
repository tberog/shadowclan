As a member

Communal property

We encourage you to share property you find, and request things you may need. Hoarding is highly discouraged. The clan makers will always provide equipment free of charge, but may be limited due to available resources.

Community

We encourage all players to group together. There is always room in a group for another clan member. Groups that reach max size will split to accomadate those who need groups. On the other hand, we do not force players to group. There are some times when a player may want to spend time by themselves.

Respect

Respect other clan members. Orders from those who are in authority are to be followed. Disregard for authority can lead to discipline by laborious tasks such as retrieving hide, bone, meat, surveying, or any other menial task the particular authority figure can contrive. Any taunts are only to be roleplayed. We encourage discussion on the message boards if you believe someone has gone overboard.

Acceptance into tribes

A member enters the clan as a gruntee. By displaying feats of honour, one may be deemed worthy to take the grunt test, whereupon passing, one pledges loyalty to the clan. Leaving the clan after such a pledge deems one a traitor, and unfit for the clan, even in other games.

Shadowclan language:

The Shadowclan language practiced in DAoC Guinevere is intended to be very easy to learn and understand (even by dum pinkys), while at the same time giving a distict non-human feel. The language is basically a minor variation of English with a little bit of "Traditional Shadowclan" vocabulary (which itself is derived largely from Professor Tolkien's Orcish & Black Speech) tossed in for good measure.

With the exception of a few basic vocabulary words, with a little practice Shadowclan language can be easily understood by simply "sounding it out" phonetically and possibly rearranging the grammer a little. The vocabulary unique to the Guinevere Clan has been intentionally minimized (compared to other Shadowclan branches) due mainly to the increased need to interact with outsiders required by DAoC on a Realm Combat enabled server.

The three Shadowclan races all speak the same language, but each race has a unique "sound" to it. These differences in dialect are also easy to follow with just a few general guidelines:

Trolls should overuse "o" and "u" and "z". Trollish is actually the baseline dialect, and Shadowclanners already familiar with the language from UO or Mordred will find trollish dialect essentially unaltered from what they speak already.

Kobolds speak much like trolls, but generally need to overuse the vowels "i" and "e" a lot more. Some kobolds sound more "trollish" than others, but all can be easily recognized by heavy use of "i" and "e".

Valkyn also sound like trolls, but add a growly/snarly quality to their speech by heavier use of "r"s and the like. To speak like a Valkyn, just follow the basics but but imagine yourself as an overgrown anthropomorphic cat to get the dialect down.

A few general tips:

Complicated pinky words or pinky placenames can alternatively "Clannified" by using enough of the word to easily convey the meaning, then replacing the rest with the suffix -blah. For example Aluminum=Alumiblah, Jordheim=Jordblah, etc.

Never use pinky shorthand, ever - unless you are trying to get yourself slow roasted in a bank of hot coals. LFG, OOC, AFK, WTB, END, LOL and the like are never to be used. When asking for a group it is acceptable to substitute the pinky-understood name for your class if you wish (ex. shaman for mojoka).

Since some respected Clan veterans will use certain abbreviations for places (in Emain, such as "G2" for the Albin Mylegayte), they can be considered exceptions to the above. At least until the revolution comes, at which time don't be stuck on the loosing side ;-)

Shadowclan must always remain in character and maintain "blah" in all open channels. The exception is in private /sends, but even then keep it to a bare minimum. Some Shadowclan won't even go ooc: in /send, so if you are getting that sort of a response unless it's urgent please be considerate and remain ic there too.

You are always free to roleplay a stupid character if you wish, but try and keep that as a seperate issue from your blah. There are truly dumb trolls, and there are some very cunning trolls too. To pinkys though, for the most part all trolls should sound alike.

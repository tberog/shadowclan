Brief History

Telara’s history is tumultuous to say the least. In ancient times, the dread gods of the Blood Storm used Telara’s proximity to planes to unleash elemental torments in hopes of plundering and destroying the world. Though they fell to infighting and were imprisoned, the Blood Storm’s creeping influence persisted through the ages, finally bearing fruit when a prince of the Mathosians did the will of Regulos, greatest of the reaver-gods.

Among the peoples of Telara, are the Bahmi. The Bahmi are descended from the Shalastiri, a race alien to the world of Telara, and of elemental planar origins. Back in the time of the first Blood Storm Wars the Shalastiri, recognizing the dangers represented by the dragons of the Blood Storm, found passage to Telara from their own home made possible through a Rift in the Plane of Air.

One tribe assembled their warriors and were led through the rift, by the great Shalastiri prince Bahmi to aid Telara in its fight against the Blood Storm. Once the Blood Storm was defeated and dragons were imprisoned, the Shalastiri found their return home blocked by the very wards they helped place.

Bahmi led the survivors of the war to settle in the Rhaza'de Canyons, overtime they interbred with the race of man, creating a new race that took on the name of their great prince, and today they are known simply as Bahmi.

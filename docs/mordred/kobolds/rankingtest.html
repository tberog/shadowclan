Tribes of Shadowclan

Shadowclan is divided into tribes, each run by one or more tribe leaders (Nobs). Nobs have authority over members of their own tribe. The tribes are united under the war leader (Wargoth) who is periodically selected in a ritual battle to the death between candidates from each tribe. The tribes are also united under the leaders of Shadowclan (Clangoths).

 

Pomdung Tribe

Pomdung tribe members (called pomdus) have just emerged from the underground to join Shadowclan on the surface. Pomdung Tribe is devoted to helping the new members adapt to the surface. It is named after Pomdung Ronkdug, the first kobold of Shadowclan to scout the surface for our invasion. Pomdung is believed to have been eaten by Njessi the sea monster.

This is the tribe for all members new to Shadowclan.

  • Minimum level to join: 1
  • Maximum level to join: 20
  • Maximum level: 25 (after level 25, Pomdus must join the Grundurz Tribe)
  • Base of operation: Fort Veldon

Due to often being in a confused state having recently emerged from underground, pomdus are considered lower in the chain of command than anyone else in Shadowclan, and should listen to what Grunds instruct them to do.

 

Grundurz Tribe

Grundurz Tribe members (called grunds) are the members of Shadowclan who, no longer unfamiliar with the surface world, are focusing on survival and becoming stronger. Grunds actively seek to make members of the clan stronger through battle. This tribe was founded by Grundurz Globuurz, who, while alone engaging a svartalf in battle, was killed by a band of wandering dwarves.

  • Minimum level to join: 10
  • Other joining requirements:
    • Former pomdung.
    • Pass Grund Test.
    • Commit to Shadowclan.
  • Maximum level: None
  • Base of operation: Yggdra Forest

 

Fotkurzmau Tribe

The Fotkurzmau Tribe is devoted to promoting group warfare over personal warfare. Its members are called Fotkurz.

  • Minimum level to join: 46+
  • Other joining requirements:
    • Former grund.
    • Cannot be a stealther class.
    • Must have Trials of Atlantis expansion for DAoC.
  • Maximum level: None

 

Bratugnag Tribe

Bratugnag is a collection of rude, crude, and abrasive Kobolds (called Brats). They are often insulting to others, especially other Brats. Their general philosophy on life is that the only way to know that you are truly close to somebody is to be able to expose all their faults, send them through the gauntlet, and still remain friends. A kobo with thin skin is most likely weak in many other ways as well, and not worthy of the harsh world we live in. As a result of this, Brats are naturally hard workers, and are very competitive. If you're going to insult another for something, you better be able to handle it yourself. Their arrogance generally leads to the attitude that if you want something done right, you do it yourself.

  • Minimum level to join: 30
  • Other joining requirements:
    • Former grund.
    • Entrance to the Bratugnag Tribe is by invitation only.
  • Maximum level: None

 

Burzulti Tribe

The Burzulti Tribe is made up of elite, tactical, and deadly kobolds of the shadows. This tribe came into being during a Wargoth Fest held at Fort Veldon. After a bloody and violent demonstration of their skills, the Burzulti looked to the Clangoth and demanded to be recognized as an official tribe. The entire clan was in shock but their request could not be ignored.

  • Minimum level to join: 45
  • Other joining requirements:
    • Former grund.
    • Hunters and Shadowblades only.
    • Must complete a series of “Bluud Trialz” to prove loyalty and skill in the ways of the shadow.
  • Maximum level: None
  • Base of Operation: Svasud Faste

 

Wargoth Fest

Occasionally, all the members of Shadowclan gather for a Wargoth Fest. Nobs from each of the senior tribes and the Grundurz Tribe submit one of their tribe members to enter the Wargoth clomp. Each of the candidates must be approved by the clangoth The candidates battle to the death along with a predetermined number of tribe mates . No outside assistance is allowed. The winner is the last one standing, and they become the Wargoth for that month, and lead the clan in battle.

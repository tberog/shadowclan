Basic Kobold Language

While hard for others to understand at times, it is actually easy to learn. 

Having lived isolated and underground for so long, it is no wonder we sound different from most kobolds.

We don't expect new members to be perfect. We do expect you to make the effort. As you practice, it will become more and more natural to you. 

We know that Shadowclan members who have just emerged from underground can be disoriented by surface life, and prone to speaking like the surface dwellers at times.


Basic Words

There are just 12 basic words that all new members should learn.  Fill in the gaps with misspelt English.  Try to avoid the letters E and C. These letters are absent from the underground kobold language, reflecting its dark and guttural nature.  

Ug hello         Hoowah! hurrah!
Gug'yu goodbye   Skah! @*&$#%!!!!
Yub yes   Har! lol, :) (smiley face)
Nub no   Rulg lat Thank you
Mih me   Blah talk, language
Lat you   Gruk know, understand

If you don't know or understand something or what someone is saying, just say Nub gruk.


Additional Vocabulary

Here are some additional words that you'll find are commonly used.

klomp attack, kill kobo kobold
mojo magic, buffs shara human
bait pull pinki non-kobold
flat dead bubhosh great, big
flishi resurrect nubhosh bad, small
lurnin experience    
uzg land, world, earth    

When you gain a level, you might say Nuu rank! or Mih hihr drumz! Other than that, kobolds don't talk much about levels and experience.

For more vocabulary, visit the Kobold Language Reference section.


Do's and Don'ts

Don't use smiley faces. Use Har!

Don't use abbreviations like lol or plz. Spell out the whole word or phrase as a Kobold would say it. For example, instead of mt (mistell), say Misblah! or Rong blah!

Don't use human numbers (1, 5, 20). If you haven't learned the Kobold numbers yet, just spell out the numbers as a Kobold would say them (wan, fiib, twinti). Kobold numbers are in the Kobold Language Reference section.

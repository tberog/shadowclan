Choosing a Kobold Name


First Names

First names must not sound meaningful in English or any other common language.

First names must not have the letters E or C. These letters are absent from the underground kobold language.

Click here for a list of example first names.


Last Names

Your last name must be an approved Shadowclan Kobold family name, and must be approved by a Nob before you go to the name registrar. You can read all about Last Names in our Member Guide.

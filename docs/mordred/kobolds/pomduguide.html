Pomdu Guide

Welcome new Pomdu into the Pomdung Tribe. You are invulnerable to PvP during this time, so use it wisely. Always listen to the nobs.

A mixture of soloing, tasks, and quests are an effective way to advance in these low levels. When hunting in groups, hunt with your fellow Shadowclan members. Head to Nisse's lair for group hunting and some decent drops. Use fort alta as your bind spot. Stay in Gotar and nearby as much as possible.


Logging into the game

If the server is full, don't use quick login. Wait at the server listing screen. Watch the population slowly drop. Once itit drops below 4000, you (and everyone else watching) can login. You only have a window of 30 seconds or so to get in, so pay attention.

Finding groups

Type /gc who to see where your fellow tribe members in your level range are. /send them a message to see if they want to group up.

Skill Distribution

Unlike other servers, you want your character to be effective at group PvP from level 10 on up.

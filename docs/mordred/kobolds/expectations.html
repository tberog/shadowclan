What We Expect of Members

Members must be in-character at all times.  The only exception is /send which can be OOC if necessary.  Being in-character is easy and has two parts - language and behavior.

Shadowclan language is described in our language sections.

Shadowclan behavior means being extremely helpful to Shadowclan members, neutral to non-Clan kobolds, and hostile to non-kobolds (especially humans, dwarves and elves).  Demand tribute of non-kobolds.  Attack known enemies or those who refuse to give tribute.  You should only group with Shadowclan members and friendly non-Shadowclan kobolds.

Members must follow the chain of command as defined in our Member Guide.

Members must choose appropriate last names as defined in our Member Guide.

Members must dye armor appropriately as defined in our Member Guide.

Members must try to not ruin the fun of others.  Only attack those who deserve to be attacked (for example - they are known enemies/traitors, they initiate combat, they refuse to pay tribute, they insult kobolds, they swear a lot).  Be respectful of fellow players both in and out of Shadowclan.

Members must never intentionally break the Terms of Service of the game or exploit bugs.

Members who have committed their character to Shadowclan must keep their character in Shadowclan.  Characters that quit the guild after making the commitment are branded traitors and attacked on sight.

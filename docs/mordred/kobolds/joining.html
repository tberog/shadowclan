Why Join Shadowclan?

You came to Mordred to battle, right?  Then fight for a cause!  Shadowclan provides a context, something worth fighting for.   It does this through an immersive roleplaying experience, a large membership community, communal sharing of equipment and supplies, members committed to helping each other out, a strong desire for group combat, and a mature membership dedicated to having fun while not ruining the fun of others.

We are open membership, so joining is easy.  Try out Shadowclan - hundreds have and hundreds have loved it!
 

Here is how you join

  1. Read about our language and what we expect of members.

  2. Make a kobold with an appropriate name, and then immediately type /gc quit. This will take you out of the newbie guild all Mordred characters start in.

  3. Log in and immediately type /who shadowclan to get a list of who is online.  /send  a message to one of them asking to be put in touch with a Nob so that you can check your name. Although the Nob will be unable to invite you until your two hour guild change timer is up, it is best to check that your name will be accepted first.

  4. Use the /played command... when it read four hours, contact a Nob to invite you. The Nob will ask a few questions to make sure you know what is expected of you and that you know some basic kobold words, than probably request you enter the zone he is in.

That's it!  Told you it was easy.

Make sure to:

Get in touch with a Nob as soon as possible, because if your name is not approved or if you are above level 20, you will have to make a new character to join Shadowclan (and this is less painful if you are low level). Click here for a list of Nob names.

Keep your safety flag on. All Shadowclan members are strongly advised to keep their safety flags on and remain PvP- as long as they can, all the way to level 10.

What We Expect of Members

Members must be in-character at all times.  The only exception is /send which can be OOC if necessary. Being in-character is easy and has two parts - language and behavior.

The Hekili language is described in our language sections.

Shadowclan behavior means being extremely helpful to Shadowclan members, neutral to non-Hekili sylvans, and hostile to non-sylvans (especially humans, dwarves, and elves). Attack known enemies of Shadowclan or those who refuse to leave sylvan territory once warned.
You should only group with Shadowclan members and friendly non-Hekili sylvans.

Members must follow the chain of command as defined in our Member Guide.

Members must choose appropriate last names as defined in our Member Guide.

Members must dye armor appropriately as defined in our Member Guide.

Members must try to not ruin the fun of others. Only attack those who deserve to be attacked (for example - they are known enemies/traitors, they initiate combat, they refuse to leave sylvan territory, they refuse to follow sylvan orders on sylvan territory, they insult sylvans, they swear a lot). Be respectful of fellow players both in and out of the Shadowclan.

Members must never intentionally break the Terms of Service of the game or exploit bugs.

Members never go /anon unless given express permission by an Ingwe.

Members who have committed their character to the Shadowclan must keep their character in the guild. Characters that quit the guild after making the commitment are branded traitors and attacked on sight.

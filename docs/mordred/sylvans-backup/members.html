Background of the Hekili

  • Sylvans are very naturally minded and value the forests (especially Hybrasil) more then anything else.
  • Sylvans are slow spoken, yet intelligent (more so then kobos).
  • The older (higher level) you are, the more respect you garnor. The oldest are considered the most wise.
  • Intense hatred of Hibernians, especially celts and elves who have built cities. Distrust/dislike of all "human" races as they are all "industrial" minded.

Traits common in the majority of Sylvans:

  • Most of the Hekili tribe are affiliated with Winter/Fall functions and beliefs, having been outcast from the Sylvan Nation, however, a few have taken on the job of Summer functions (see Tribes).
  • Many Sylvans have an intense dislike/fear of axes. Many others hate hammers and bows because of their noncombat uses and how they were created.
  • Many Sylvans flat out refuse to talk to non sylvans (other than kobo now) at all.

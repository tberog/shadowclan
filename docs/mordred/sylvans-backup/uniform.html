Tribe Uniforms:
Each tribe has their own uniform which shows their growth and role within the Hekili.

A member of the Tulie tribe is colored all in brown signifying the soil they come from.
A Tulie Ingwe also wears a green cloak.

Members of the Laire tribe wear blue gloves and boots, signifying bardic heritage and the vast expanses of sky and water from which they gather their inspiration.
A Laire Ingwe would also wear a blue cloak.

Members of the Yavie tribe wear orange boots and gloves to signify the autumnal spirit they gain their strength from.
A Yavie Ingwe also wears an orange cloak.

Members of the Hrive tribe wear grey boots and gloves signifying the death and harshness of winter.
A Hrive Ingwe would also wear a grey cloak.

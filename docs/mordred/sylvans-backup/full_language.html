Language

The Sylvan language is a hybrid of Quenya (the preferred language of the Ents (other then entish which is undocumented)) and Black Speech/Koboblah. Essentially "c"'s in Quenya are usually pronounced as "k"'s so any "c"'s have been changed to "k"s. The number system is the same as the kobold one, only elongating the vowels. The Sylvans acknowledge standard Quenya as their "Ancient Sylvan" and the new language as their common tongue. Common is used almost exclusively, though their may be Sylvan ceremonies and such that will dig deeper into the ancient language.

In an effort to tie back into Entish and a way to easily delineate which race is talking, sentences use dashes (-) instead of spaces.
e.g.:
human: Lets go kill stuff.
kobo: Litz gu klomp stuv.
sylvan: Let-nia-goo-upruut-stuuf.

As far as speech patterns for non specific words, the Sylvans use as many double vowels as possible (every sentence other than one word utterances should have atleast one double vowel), and refrain from using c (c sound is not really used in the language). "A"'s are heavily used. In addition to this they pluralize almost exclusively with "i" in the case of words ending with a consonant and "r" when the word ends in a vowel, as this is consistent with actual Quenya language rules. Sylvans have a great fascination for language and know many languages, because of this they often preface long sentences with "Aas-yi-kobor-wouuld-blaah, <insert sentence in standard kobo>".
